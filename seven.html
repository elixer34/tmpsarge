Our first single was released by Mud Records in March 1996. It features the songs "Dear Josie, Love Robyn" and "The Last Boy."

Our first drummer Russ and I designed the cover and forgot to put the names of the songs anywhere on the sleeve. oops.


Our second single came out in August 1996 and was put out by our friends in Braid with their label Grand Theft Autumn. It's a split single with the band Supporting Actress, who put the song "I'm Attracted to..." on their side. Our side has the song "Backlash," and I don't know who did the artwork for our side, but the girl looks so sad...


Our third single came out in May 1997, on Mud Records. The A side is one of our new songs "Stall" and the B side is a cover of the Cyndi Lauper tune "Time After Time." We recorded these songs with Matt Talbott, who's in Hum and who built a cool recording studio in the basement of his home. My friend Mike Herbert did the design and illustrations, which are actaully purple and green, but the file I have on my server is different...
